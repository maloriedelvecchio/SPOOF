Our Project

SPOOF! is brought to you by four passionate Interaction Design students from George Brown College who will be entering their third and final year in September of 2023. We were tasked with creating an installation for an imagined "Fun Palace" exhibit that would bring community members together for our Physical Interfaces class. Drawing on our expertise and creativity, we crafted a memorable game-like experience where people could connect, bond, and share lighthearted fun through goofy gestures and even more wacky guesses. Each step of the way, we were guided by our intended audience and purpose to build a unique and captivating immersive experience. We hope that you enjoy playing it as much as we enjoyed making it!
